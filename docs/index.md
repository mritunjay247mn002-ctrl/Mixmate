# Privacy Policy for MixMate

**Effective date:** April 17, 2026

MixMate ("we", "our", or "us") is a cocktail and mocktail recipe mobile application.  
This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

MixMate does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal identifiers such as your name, phone number, or home address.

If advertising is enabled in the app, third-party advertising partners may automatically process limited device and usage data to provide, personalize, and measure ads. This may include:

- Device identifiers (for example, Advertising ID)
- Basic device and app information (OS version, app version, language)
- Approximate location inferred from IP address
- Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks)

## 2. How the App Uses Data

MixMate uses data for:

- Core app functionality (favorites, recently viewed drinks, local preferences, audio playback)
- Displaying ads and improving ad relevance/performance when ads are enabled
- Maintaining app performance and reliability

Most app feature data (favorites, history, preferences) is stored locally on your device.

## 3. Data Sharing

MixMate does not sell your personal data.  
Data may be shared with advertising partners only to the extent required to serve, personalize, and measure ads.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Local app data (favorites/history/preferences) remains on your device.
- Reasonable safeguards are used through platform-standard controls.
- No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 5. Third-Party Services

When ads are enabled, MixMate may use third-party advertising SDKs (such as Google AdMob or similar providers). These providers may process device and app data under their own privacy policies.

Relevant policies include:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Ad Technology information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## 6. Children's Privacy

MixMate is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

## 7. International Data Transfers

If third-party advertising services are used, data may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to the provider's safeguards and legal terms.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any update will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
