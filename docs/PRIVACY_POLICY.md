# Privacy Policy for MixMate

**Effective date:** April 17, 2026

MixMate ("we", "our", or "us") is an offline cocktail and mocktail recipe mobile application.  
This Privacy Policy explains how MixMate handles data when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

MixMate does **not** collect personal information from users.

- No account or login is required.
- No name, email, phone number, or address is collected.
- No payment information is collected.
- No device contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or location data are collected.

## 2. How the App Uses Data

MixMate stores limited app data **locally on your device only** to provide core offline features, such as:

- favorite drinks
- recently viewed drinks
- local app preferences
- local audio playback state

This data is not transmitted to MixMate servers or third parties.

## 3. Data Sharing

MixMate does **not** share user data with any third party.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All app data remains on the user's device.
- MixMate does not operate a backend server for user data storage.
- Because data is local-only, uninstalling the app may remove local app data.

## 5. Third-Party Services

MixMate does **not** use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs for personal data collection.

## 6. Children's Privacy

MixMate does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any users.

## 7. International Data Transfers

MixMate does not transfer personal data across borders because no personal data is collected or transmitted.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future if app functionality changes (for example, if account, cloud sync, analytics, or ads are added).  
Any update will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
